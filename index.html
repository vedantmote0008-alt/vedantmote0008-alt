About Us

Our tea journey started with a simple love for chai and a dream to share it across Maharashtra. We noticed how tea is not just a drink here—it’s a part of daily life, a moment to connect with friends and family. Inspired by this, we founded Divyadiksha Matrtulya Chaha, bringing authentic, flavorful teas to every cup.

From experimenting with unique blends in small batches to sharing them with local communities, our passion grew into a vision: to spread warmth, happiness, and the perfect cup of chai all over Maharashtra. Today, our franchise helps tea lovers everywhere enjoy this experience.
